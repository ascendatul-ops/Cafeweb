Our Story

More Than
Just a Café

Welcome to Chill with Atul — a cozy corner where the warmth of Indian hospitality meets the laid-back vibes of your favourite café. Born from a passion for authentic flavours and meaningful connections, we've created a space where every chai tells a story.

Our menu celebrates the rich tapestry of Indian street food and snacks — from crispy samosas to aromatic masala chai — all crafted with love and the finest ingredients.
